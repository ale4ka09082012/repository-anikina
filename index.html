SERGEY LAZAREV

Sergey Lazarev is a singer, actor and TV presenter. Former member of the pop group Smash!!.

Basic information:

Full name : Sergey Vyacheslavovich Lazarev

Date of Birth: April 1, 1983 (age 40)

Place of Birth: Moscow, USSR

Professions: singer, actor, TV presenter.

Years of activity: 2000 - now

Singing voice: tenor

Tools : piano

He performed twice at the international Eurovision Song Contest (in 2016 in Stockholm and in 2019 in Tel Aviv), taking 2nd place both times.

Winner of the Golden Gramophone Award; multiple participant, and since 2007, host of the annual Song of the Year festival. In 2021, he took part in the “Dancing with the Stars” competition and took 1st place. In 2023 he became the winner of the “Mask” show.
